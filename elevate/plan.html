Trip Essentials

Everything You Need to Know

1 🚗

Getting Here

Cloudcroft is nestled in the Sacramento Mountains of southern New Mexico.

  • From El Paso, TX — ~2 hours (130 mi)
  • From Las Cruces, NM — ~1.5 hours (85 mi)
  • From Albuquerque, NM — ~3.5 hours (215 mi)
  • From Alamogordo, NM — ~30 min (20 mi)

Take US-82 East from Alamogordo — a stunning mountain drive climbing 4,000 feet in 16 miles.

2 🌤

Weather & When to Visit

Cloudcroft averages 200+ days of sunshine with four distinct seasons.

Spring 55–70°F
Summer 70–82°F
Fall 55–68°F
Winter 38–50°F

Current Conditions

Summer highs rarely exceed 82°F — a cool escape when the desert hits 100°+.

3 🎒

What to Pack

Altitude essentials: sunscreen (UV is stronger up here), layers (temps drop 20° at sunset), sturdy shoes, and water.

  • Summer: Rain jacket for afternoon storms
  • Winter: Warm layers + snow gear
  • All seasons: Sunglasses
4 🛒

Groceries & Supplies

Stock up before you arrive or find essentials in the village.

Cloudcroft has limited grocery options — consider stopping in Alamogordo for a full grocery run.

Local Markets

Alamogordo Walmart

5 🚨

Emergency Info

Emergency: 911

Otero County Sheriff: (575) 437-2210

Gerald Champion Hospital (Alamogordo): (575) 439-6100

Nearest hospital is 30 min downhill in Alamogordo.

6 💡

Local Tips

  • Gas up in Alamogordo — fuel options are limited in Cloudcroft.
  • Cell service can be spotty in the forest — download offline maps.
  • Afternoon thunderstorms are common in summer — plan outdoor activities for mornings.
  • Wildlife is abundant — store food properly and give elk the right of way.
