Groceries & Bakeries

Stocking Up in the Mountains

Plan ahead for your mountain getaway. While Cloudcroft doesn't have a full grocery store, local markets and nearby options keep you well-supplied.

💡

Pro Tip: There's no full-scale grocery store in Cloudcroft. Stock up in Alamogordo (20 miles) before heading up the mountain, or use the local markets for essentials.

In Cloudcroft

Local Markets & Essentials

Convenience stores and specialty shops in the village offer basics and unique local finds.

🏪

Convenience & Basics

Allsup's Market

Convenience store with gas, frozen and deli meats, milk, eggs, dry and canned goods. Also carries fresh cuts of meat.

Gas Groceries Fresh Meat

Family Dollar

Discount groceries including cooking and baking supplies, milk, eggs, bread, and toiletries. Located at 309 James Canyon Highway.

Discount Essentials Toiletries

The Weed Store

Located on Agua Chiquita Road, carrying grocery goods, diesel and unleaded fuel.

Gas Groceries

Bakeries & Coffee

Eight the Cake

Specialty & Fresh Food

Club Veg at Instant Karma

Nearby Options

Full Grocery Stores in Alamogordo

For complete grocery shopping, head down the mountain to Alamogordo. Some stores even deliver to Cloudcroft.

🛒

Walmart

~20 miles

Full-service Walmart Supercenter with grocery, pharmacy, and household goods. Delivers to Cloudcroft via app.

🥗

Albertsons Market

~20 miles

1300 E 10th St, Alamogordo. Full grocery with bakery, deli, meat, and produce departments.

🏠

Walmart Neighborhood Market

~20 miles

On Indian Wells in Alamogordo. Delivers to Cloudcroft including pharmacy prescriptions via the app.

🔨

Lowe's

~20 miles

A favorite among mountain toppers for cabin supplies, hardware, propane, firewood, and home improvement essentials. Stock up on everything you need before heading up the hill.

📋
