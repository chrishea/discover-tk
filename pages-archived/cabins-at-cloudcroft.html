A Mountain Treasure Since 1965

The Cabins at Cloudcroft is a beloved retreat nestled in the Sacramento Mountains within Lincoln National Forest. Built in 1965, our cabins maintain their nostalgic charm with knotty pine walls and ceilings, original metal cabinet kitchens, and that authentic 1950s mountain lodge feel.

"A treasure! Everything you would expect from a mountain cabin—cozy fireplace, retro-lodge nostalgic decor, SUPER CLEAN."

Owners Karl and Laura go out of their way to make every stay delightful. Within walking distance to Burro Street's shops and restaurants, and close to hiking trails and Ski Cloudcroft, the cabins provide an ideal home base for your mountain adventure.
