A Cloudcroft Tradition

Since 1950, Western Bar & Cafe has been the heart of Cloudcroft dining. This family-owned establishment has served generations of locals and visitors with the same commitment to quality and hospitality. From our famous all-day breakfast to hand-pattied burgers and fresh-cut fries, every dish is made with care. Whether you're fueling up for a mountain adventure or winding down after a day on the trails, our cozy cafe feels like home.
