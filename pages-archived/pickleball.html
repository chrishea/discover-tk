Pickleball in the Clouds

Pickleball has taken Cloudcroft by storm, becoming one of the village's most popular recreational activities. On any given Tuesday, Thursday, or Saturday morning, you'll find three dozen players on the courts at Zenith Park, batting yellow balls back and forth in the crisp mountain air.

The cool summer climate makes Cloudcroft an ideal destination for outdoor pickleball when temperatures in the lowlands become unbearable. Whether you're a seasoned player or picking up a paddle for the first time, the welcoming pickleball community is ready to help you get in the game.

Pickleball at Zenith Park

Where to Play

Zenith Park Tennis Courts

Cloudcroft's public pickleball courts are located at Zenith Park on Highway 82.

Zenith Park

Highway 82, Cloudcroft, NM 88317

🎾

Number of Courts

3 outdoor courts with permanent lines

💰

Cost

Free to play — no reservations needed

🕐

Hours

Open 24/7, seven days a week

🎒

What to Bring

Bring your own net, paddles, and balls

Note: The courts have permanent pickleball lines, but you'll need to bring your own portable net. Local players often have extra equipment and are happy to share!

Join the Fun

Pickleball Addicts of Cloudcroft

👥

Active Community

The PAC (Pickleball Addicts of Cloudcroft) brings together locals and visitors for regular play sessions throughout the week.

🎓

Clinics & Lessons

Public clinics and classes are offered for players of all skill levels. Beginners are always welcome!

🏆

Tournaments

The annual "Pickleball in the Clouds" tournament draws 70+ players for competitive fun in the mountains.

Open Play Schedule

Tuesday Morning open play

Times may vary seasonally. Courts are open 24/7 for casual play outside of scheduled sessions. Connect with local players at the courts for the latest schedule and to get involved!

Why Cloudcroft

The Perfect Place to Play

🌡️

Cool Mountain Air

While lowland courts sizzle in summer heat, Cloudcroft's 9,000-foot elevation keeps temperatures comfortable for outdoor play all season long.

🌲

Scenic Setting

Play surrounded by towering pines and fresh mountain air. Every game comes with views of the beautiful Sacramento Mountains.

🤝

Friendly Players

The pickleball community welcomes players of all levels. Whether you're a beginner or advanced, you'll find partners ready to play.

🏔️

Vacation & Play

Combine your mountain getaway with your favorite sport. Cloudcroft offers great lodging, dining, and hiking alongside great pickleball.

Ready to Play?

Grab your paddle and head to Zenith Park for the best pickleball experience in the mountains. See you on the courts!
