Our Story

Who We Are

Passionate about sharing the magic of New Mexico's beloved mountain village with visitors from around the world

Our Mission

Sharing the Mountain Magic

Discover Cloudcroft was created with one simple goal: to help visitors experience everything that makes this mountain village special.

Cloudcroft isn't just a destination - it's a feeling. That first breath of cool pine-scented air as you crest the mountain. The way the Milky Way seems close enough to touch on a clear night. The friendly wave from locals who genuinely want you to love their town as much as they do.

We created Discover Cloudcroft because we believe everyone deserves to experience this magic. Whether you're escaping the desert heat for a weekend, planning your dream mountain wedding, or considering making these mountains your forever home, we want to be your trusted guide to everything Cloudcroft has to offer.

Our Mission

To be the most helpful, comprehensive, and welcoming resource for anyone wanting to visit, explore, or become part of the Cloudcroft community - connecting visitors with the experiences, businesses, and people that make this mountain village extraordinary.

What We Do

Your Mountain Resource

From planning your first visit to finding your dream cabin, we're here to help at every step of your Cloudcroft journey.

For Visitors: We provide comprehensive guides to lodging, dining, activities, and events. Our goal is to help you make the most of every moment in the mountains, whether you have a weekend or a week.

For Future Residents: Thinking about buying property or relocating? We offer honest information about what it's really like to live in Cloudcroft - the joys, the challenges, and everything in between.

For Local Businesses: We shine a spotlight on the restaurants, shops, and services that make Cloudcroft special. If you're a local business owner who wants to be featured, we'd love to hear from you.

For the Community: We work to promote events, share important local news, and help strengthen the connections that make small-town life so meaningful.

Our Community

Part of Something Special

A Village Like No Other

Cloudcroft has always been a place where people come to escape, to breathe, to reconnect with nature and each other. Since 1899, visitors have been falling in love with these mountains - and many have never left.

Today, our small village of about 750 year-round residents welcomes thousands of visitors each year. We're artists and astronomers, retirees and remote workers, families who've been here for generations and newcomers who arrived last month. What unites us is a deep appreciation for this special place and a genuine desire to share it with others.
