Golf at The Lodge

Play one of America's highest and oldest golf courses! The Lodge Resort's 18-hole championship course has welcomed golfers since 1899, offering stunning mountain views and a unique high-altitude challenge. At 9,000 feet, watch your drives fly farther in the thin mountain air.

⛳ 18 Holes ⛰️ 9,000 ft Elevation 📅 Est. 1899 🏛️ Scottish Tradition

The course is governed by Scottish tradition and offers a blend of mountain charm with challenging play. Cart and club rentals available. Pro shop on site.

🥏

Disc Golf

Cloudcroft Community Disc Golf Course is ranked in the top 10 courses in New Mexico! This free 18-hole course at 230 Mescalero Avenue offers a challenging round through beautiful pine forest. Dog-friendly and open to all skill levels.

🥏 18 Holes ⭐ 4.6 Rating (506 reviews) 🆓 Free to Play 🐕 Dog Friendly

Rated "Hard" difficulty, the course covers about 2 miles and takes approximately 2 hours to complete. Bring your own discs—no rentals on site.

🏓

Pickleball

America's fastest-growing sport has found a home in the Sacramento Mountains! Zenith Park Tennis Courts offers 3 dedicated pickleball courts for drop-in play. The Cloudcroft Resort also features pickleball opportunities for guests.

🏓 3 Courts at Zenith Park 📍 Village Location 🎾 Resort Courts Available

Note: At 9,000 feet, allow time to acclimate before intense play. The thin air can affect your game and stamina. Bring your own paddles and balls.
