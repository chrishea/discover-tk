Celebrate Cloudcroft's Logging Heritage

Every September, Cloudcroft's Zenith Park transforms into a celebration of the Sacramento Mountains' rich logging heritage. Lumberjack Day brings together skilled competitors, local artisans, and thousands of visitors for a day of old-fashioned mountain fun. Watch axe throwers demonstrate their precision, cheer on chainsaw competitors, and enjoy a hearty pancake breakfast under the pines. This family-friendly festival honors the lumberjacks who helped build our mountain community while creating new memories for generations to come.

Event Schedule

Day of Events

Plan your day at Lumberjack Day with our event schedule. Arrive early for the pancake breakfast and stay for all the action.

7:30 AM - 9:30 AM

Pancake Breakfast

Start your day with a hearty mountain breakfast. All-you-can-eat pancakes served fresh under the pines.

8:00 AM

Competitor Registration

Registration opens for all lumberjack competition categories. Sign up early to secure your spot.

9:00 AM

Competitions Begin

The main events kick off with axe throwing, chainsaw competitions, and single buck sawing contests.

All Day

Vendors & Exhibitors

Browse over 40 exhibitors featuring local crafts, art, and mountain goods. Eight food booths offer a variety of treats.

Throughout Day

Kids Activities

Special competitions and activities designed for young lumberjacks. Family-friendly fun for all ages.

Afternoon

Awards Ceremony

Celebrate the champions as winners are announced for all competition categories.

Main Events

Lumberjack Competitions

Watch skilled competitors battle it out in traditional lumberjack events. Both men's and women's divisions compete.

🪓

Axe Throwing

Precision meets power in this classic lumberjack sport. Watch competitors hit their marks with deadly accuracy.

⛓️

Chainsaw Competition

Speed and skill combine as competitors race through logs with roaring chainsaws in timed events.

🪚

Single Buck Sawing

A test of endurance and technique using traditional crosscut saws. Pure old-school lumberjack action.

👧

Kids Competition

Young competitors get their chance to shine in age-appropriate challenges that teach lumberjack skills.
