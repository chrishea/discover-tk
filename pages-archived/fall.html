Fall in Cloudcroft

Golden Aspens Paint the Mountains

Autumn transforms the Sacramento Mountains into a spectacular canvas of gold, orange, and crimson. Crisp mountain air, harvest festivals, and stunning foliage make fall one of our most magical seasons.

🌸 Spring ☀️ Summer 🍂 Fall ❄️ Winter

Peak Foliage Season

Fall in Cloudcroft is a photographer's dream. The aspen groves turn brilliant gold against the deep green of the ponderosa pines, creating a contrast that draws visitors from across the Southwest.

With comfortable daytime temperatures and cool evenings perfect for bonfires, fall offers ideal conditions for hiking, scenic drives, and outdoor exploration. The summer crowds have thinned, but the beauty is at its peak.

Fall Activities

Things to Do This Season

From leaf-peeping drives to harvest festivals, fall in Cloudcroft offers unforgettable experiences.

🍁

Fall Foliage Drives

The Sunspot Scenic Byway and Cloud Climbing Railroad Trestle offer spectacular fall color viewing. Peak color typically arrives mid-to-late October.

🥾

Autumn Hiking

Cool temperatures and stunning colors make fall perfect for hiking. The Rim Trail and Osha Trail pass through prime aspen groves.

🎃

Harvest Festivals

Pumpkin patches, hayrides, costume contests, and autumn treats. Cloudcroft's Harvest Fest is a beloved annual tradition.

📸

Photography Tours

The contrast of golden aspens against evergreen pines creates stunning photography opportunities around every corner.

🍷

Wine Tasting

Warm up with local wines at Noisy Water Winery. Their famous green chile wine pairs perfectly with crisp fall days.

🦌

Wildlife Viewing

Fall is prime time for wildlife sightings. Elk are active during the rut, and deer are frequently spotted throughout the village.

What to Expect

Fall Weather

🌤️

Sunny Days

Fall typically brings clear, sunny days perfect for outdoor activities. The famous New Mexico blue sky is at its best.

🧥

Cool Mornings

Mornings can be chilly, especially in late October. Layer up and bring a warm jacket for early starts.

❄️

First Snow

Late October or November often brings the first snowfall. Check conditions if traveling during this transition time.

Fall Events

Celebrate the Season

October

Harvest Fest

Cloudcroft's biggest fall celebration features pumpkin patches, hayrides, costume contests, live music, and autumn treats for the whole family.

October

Aspenfest Art Walk

Local galleries open their doors for a special evening of art, wine, and fall-inspired exhibits along Burro Avenue.

November

Veterans Day Celebration

The community honors our veterans with a parade and ceremony in this patriotic small-town tradition.

November

Thanksgiving Weekend

Escape to the mountains for a peaceful holiday. Local restaurants offer special Thanksgiving menus and the village is beautifully decorated.
