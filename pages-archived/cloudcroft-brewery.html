Brewed at Altitude

Cloudcroft Brewery brings the craft beer revolution to the Sacramento Mountains with a rotating selection of handcrafted ales, lagers, and seasonal specialties. Our brewmaster uses the pure mountain water and cool climate to create exceptional brews that capture the spirit of the high country. Pair your pint with our famous brick-oven pizzas, made fresh with local ingredients, and enjoy the relaxed mountain atmosphere of our taproom and outdoor beer garden.
