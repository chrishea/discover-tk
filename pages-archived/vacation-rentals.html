Booking Tips

Get the Best Stay

📅

Book Early

Peak season (June-July, December) fills up fast. Reserve 2-3 months ahead for best selection.

💰

Off-Peak Deals

February, October, and November offer lower rates and fewer crowds.

🔥

Check Amenities

Look for fireplaces, heating, and hot tubs—mountain nights get cold!

📶

WiFi & Cell

97% have WiFi, but cell service varies. Ask hosts about connectivity.

🍳

Full Kitchen

97% have kitchens. Stock up on groceries in Alamogordo before arrival.

🅿️

Parking

92% offer parking. Confirm driveway accessibility for larger vehicles.

🐕

Pet Policies

Many are pet-friendly, but check size limits and pet fees before booking.

⭐

Read Reviews

Recent reviews are gold. Check for cleanliness, accuracy, and host responsiveness.
