A Serene Mountain Escape

Summit Inn offers a peaceful retreat in the heart of Cloudcroft, just 0.1 miles from the village center and steps away from Lincoln National Forest. Whether you're seeking adventure on the trails or relaxation by the fireplace, Summit Inn provides the perfect home base for your mountain getaway.

Each accommodation features a cozy stone fireplace, fully-equipped kitchenette, and all the comforts you need for a memorable stay. Wake up to continental breakfast, spend your days exploring the mountains, and wind down by the outdoor fire pit under the starry New Mexico sky.
