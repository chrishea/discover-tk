Winter in Cloudcroft

The Southernmost Ski Area in the U.S.

Snow-dusted pines, crackling fireplaces, and the magic of a mountain winter await. Cloudcroft transforms into a winter wonderland perfect for skiing, tubing, snowmobiling, and cozy cabin getaways.

🌸 Spring ☀️ Summer 🍂 Fall ❄️ Winter

A Mountain Winter Wonderland

Winter in Cloudcroft offers something rare in the Southwest: real snow, real skiing, and real winter magic. Ski Apache and local snow activities bring visitors seeking a mountain winter experience without traveling to Colorado.

But winter isn't just about the slopes. Cozy up in a cabin with a crackling fire, enjoy hot chocolate at the ice rink, or take a quiet snowshoe hike through the frozen forest. This is winter the way it should be.

Winter Activities

Things to Do This Season

From the slopes to the fireside, winter in Cloudcroft offers endless ways to embrace the season.

⛷️

Skiing & Snowboarding

Ski Apache, about an hour away, offers the southernmost skiing in the US with 55 trails and 750 acres of skiable terrain.

🛷

Snow Tubing

Family-friendly snow tubing runs offer thrills without the learning curve. Pure winter fun for all ages.

⛸️

Ice Skating

The Southwest's only natural outdoor ice rink opens in December. Skate under the stars with hot chocolate in hand.

🏔️

Snowmobiling

Explore the winter backcountry on guided snowmobile tours through the Lincoln National Forest.

🥾

Snowshoeing

Experience the quiet beauty of the winter forest on snowshoe. Trails throughout Lincoln National Forest offer peaceful exploration.

🔥

Cozy Cabin Time

There's nothing like a roaring fire, a good book, and a hot drink after a day in the snow. Our cabins are made for winter.

What to Expect

Winter Weather

🌨️

Snow Events

Cloudcroft receives an average of 80 inches of snow annually. Significant storms can drop 6-12 inches at a time.

🚗

Road Conditions

Highway 82 can be icy or snow-packed. Check road conditions before traveling and carry chains just in case.

☀️

Sunny Days

New Mexico's famous blue sky still shines in winter. Many days are crisp, clear, and perfect for outdoor activities.

Winter Events

Celebrate the Season

December

Christmas in the Clouds

The village transforms into a winter wonderland with holiday lights, tree lighting ceremony, caroling, Santa visits, and a festive holiday market.

December 31

New Year's Eve Celebration

Ring in the new year at 9,000 feet. Local restaurants and venues host special celebrations to welcome the year ahead.

February

Valentine's Weekend

A romantic mountain getaway. Cozy cabins, candlelit dinners, and snowy landscapes make Cloudcroft perfect for couples.

All Winter

Ice Skating Season

The outdoor ice rink operates December through February, weather permitting. Skate rentals available.
