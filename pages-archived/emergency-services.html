Emergency Services in Cloudcroft

Know your resources before you need them. Local volunteer teams and regional services ensure help is available in the Sacramento Mountains.

🚑

Emergency Medical Services

Multiple ambulance services respond to the Cloudcroft area. For life-threatening emergencies, patients are transported to the nearest appropriate hospital.

🏥
AMR (American Medical Response) Paid staff covering all of Otero County
🚒
Volunteer Fire Dept EMS Local volunteers providing vital life-saving care
⏱️
Response Time Varies by location; remote areas may take longer
🚒

Fire Department

The Cloudcroft Volunteer Fire Department provides fire suppression, rescue, and emergency medical response throughout the village and surrounding areas.

📍
Cloudcroft VFD Volunteer firefighters serving the community
🌲
Wildfire Response Coordination with US Forest Service for forest fires
👮

Law Enforcement

Law enforcement services are provided by multiple agencies depending on jurisdiction and nature of the incident.

🏛️
Otero County Sheriff Primary law enforcement for unincorporated areas
🛡️
NM State Police Highway patrol and state jurisdiction
Nearest Hospitals

Emergency Rooms

The nearest emergency room is in Alamogordo, approximately 27 minutes from Cloudcroft. For life-threatening emergencies, call 911.

CHRISTUS Health - Alamogordo

📍 ~27 minutes from Cloudcroft

99-bed Level III Trauma Center providing 24-hour emergency care. The largest healthcare facility in Otero County, serving the community since 1949.

🏥 24/7 Emergency Room

Important Information

📱 Cell Service is Limited

Cell coverage is spotty throughout the Sacramento Mountains. If you can't get a signal, try moving to higher ground or driving toward the village center. Know your location before you need help.

🏔️ Remote Area Response

Communities like Timberon are over an hour from the nearest ER. Consider your distance from emergency services when planning remote activities.

🚑 Transport Decisions

For life-threatening emergencies, ambulances transport to the nearest hospital. For non-life-threatening situations, you may be taken to your hospital of choice.

👥 Volunteer Responders

Many mountain area EMS responders are volunteers. Your patience is appreciated as these dedicated community members provide critical services.
